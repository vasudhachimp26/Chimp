My Hobbie is Doing Art

Art is my hobbie. I like art because you can use your imagination to paint whatever you want. They are a lots of ways to make art. Some types of painting are acrylic painting, oil painting, and watercolor painting. I like to do acrylic painting. Some ways of drawing can be used with pens, pencils, and ink pens, and markers.

Art is something you can do to be creative and fun. Art can also be very relaxing to do!
